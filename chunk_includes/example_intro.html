In this experiment, you will be required to read a number of incomplete English sentences one word at a time and complete these sentences. You can advance to the next word by pressing the space bar.

When you get to the end of the sentence on the screen, you will be asked to choose between two words to complete the sentence. Please click on the button that completes the sentence.

The data collected in this experiment will be used for the purposes of a class project for UChicago course LING 30401 Psycholinguistics. Your personal data will only be used for validation and payment purposes.
